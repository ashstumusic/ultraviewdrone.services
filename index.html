Meet the Owner & Pilot

Your Local Drone Recovery Expert

Based in Claremore, Oklahoma, the owner and pilot of Ultra View Drone Recovery brings a passion for the land and a dedication to helping ranchers, farmers, and pet owners find what matters most. With FAA certification and years of flight experience, every mission is conducted with precision and care.

Whether you've lost livestock across rugged terrain or a beloved pet has gone missing, this service was built around one goal: getting eyes in the sky fast so you can get answers on the ground.

  • FAA Part 107 Certified Drone Pilot
  • Based in Claremore, OK — covering Northeast Oklahoma
  • Thermal & standard aerial imaging
  • Available for emergency and scheduled recoveries

Our Mission & Services

"When animals go missing, every hour counts. Ultra View Drone Recovery puts advanced aerial technology to work for Oklahoma's ranchers, farmers, and pet owners — delivering fast, professional recovery services when you need them most."

Livestock Recovery

Locate missing or escaped cattle, deer, and large animals across open fields, dense brush, and rough terrain faster than any ground search.

Pet Recovery

Missing dogs, cats, or other pets can cover surprising ground. We deploy quickly to cover neighborhoods, fields, and wooded areas with aerial eyes.

Aerial Search & Assist

Real-time aerial footage streamed to help ground teams navigate. We coordinate with you directly to guide searchers toward confirmed sightings.

Property & Range Scouting

Pre-emptive aerial surveys of your property to identify fence gaps, water crossings, and vulnerable perimeters before animals go missing.

Our Equipment

Professional-grade gear built for the job

Professional Drone Platform

Long-range, high-stability drone capable of covering large tracts of land. Extended flight time for thorough search coverage.

Thermal Imaging Camera

Detects heat signatures through brush, tall grass, and low-light conditions. Ideal for locating animals at dawn, dusk, or after dark.

Live Video Feed

Real-time HD video streamed to the ground so you can see exactly what the drone sees — no guessing, no waiting for post-flight review.
